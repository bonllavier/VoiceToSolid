Why VoiceToSolid?

The first stage was a brainstorm, based on finding solutions to people's daily problems, through an application, we proceeded to analyze each of the proposals. But in the end we decided to try to develop something a little more experimental, and achievable, where you can apply the areas of interest of each of the members. And that is VoiceToSolid .
